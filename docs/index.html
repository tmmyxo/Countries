Terratorries

About the app

This app allows you to browse countries and other territories of the world, see basic data about them, and view their locations on the world map. You can play a mini-game to memorize flags of countries you're interested in, and finally, you have a MapView that allows you to save locations by placing a point and giving it a name. You can also save your current location as a point and see a short or full address of the selected point in a pop-over view.

Technologies used

  • Swift
  • UIKit
  • WebKit
  • MapKit
  • CoreData
